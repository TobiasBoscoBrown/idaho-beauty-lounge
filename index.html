Our story

A studio built on precision, warmth, and real results

Idaho Beauty Lounge began with a simple belief: that great skin, lashes, and brows should come from real training and genuine care. Owner Kristina grew up in Ukraine, moved to Idaho at 13, and built her craft on Eastern European beauty standards known for precision and quality. Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How long does a Korean lash lift last?

A lash lift and tint lasts about 6 to 8 weeks, which is your natural lash growth cycle. There is no daily upkeep, just avoid steam and oil based products for the first 24 hours.

Lash lift or lash extensions, which is right for me?

A lash lift enhances your own lashes for a soft, low maintenance look with no fills. Extensions add length and fullness for a more dramatic, makeup free look and need refills every 2 to 3 weeks. We will help you choose at your consultation.

What is brow lamination and how long does it last?

Brow lamination is a keratin treatment that lifts and sets your brow hairs into a fuller, brushed up shape. It lasts 6 to 8 weeks, and our Brow Sculpt package adds mapping, shaping, and a tint for a finished look.

Do you treat acne?

Yes. Our owner is a Face Reality Certified Acne Specialist, so we build a real acne clearing plan with in studio treatments and a home care routine, recommended every 2 to 3 weeks for visible results.

How do I book, and is a deposit required?

Booking is online through our scheduling page, available 24 hours a day. New clients leave a small deposit that goes toward the service. Please reschedule at least 48 hours ahead.
