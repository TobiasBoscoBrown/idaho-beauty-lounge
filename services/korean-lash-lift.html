Korean Lash Lift & Tint

A gentle keratin lift that curls and lifts your natural lashes, with a rich tint, for 6 to 8 weeks of effortless, wide-awake eyes. No extensions, no mascara.

What it is

A Korean keratin lash lift reshapes your own lashes from the base, lifting and curling them upward, then a tint adds depth and color for a fuller, darker look. The result is soft, natural, and lasts 6 to 8 weeks with zero daily effort. It is the signature service our clients drive across the valley for.

Good for

  • Anyone who wants a low maintenance, mascara free look
  • Lashes that point straight or down and need lift
  • Clients who want a softer alternative to extensions
  • Brides, travelers, and busy moms

What's included

  • Lash and eye consultation
  • Gentle keratin lift on your natural lashes
  • Lash tint for richer color
  • Nourishing aftercare guidance
